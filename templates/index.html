Awesome Work

The Therapeia Chatbot represents a groundbreaking fusion of artificial intelligence and therapeutic principles, designed to address the increasing demand for mental health support. In an era where mental health challenges are escalating, and timely assistance is crucial, Therapeia offers an innovative solution: a digital companion providing therapeutic interactions. This paper delves into the design, functionality, and underlying technologies of the chatbot.
